Current Leadership

Syed Sibghatullah Shah Rashdi III, the 8th Pir Pagaro, continues to uphold the noble lineage of spiritual guidance, national service, and social reform. Under his visionary leadership, the Hur Jamaat has transitioned into a modern era while staying deeply rooted in its historical traditions of loyalty and sacrifice.

His tenure is marked by a profound emphasis on educational advancement and strict community discipline. He has been instrumental in organizing the Jamaat into a more structured social force, ensuring that the message of peace, patriotism, and spiritual enlightenment reaches every follower. As the guardian of the Hur Mujahid Force, he remains a symbol of unity and a beacon of hope for millions across the globe.
