The Spiritual Legacy

Understanding the Role and Impact of the Pir Pagaro and the Hur Jamaat.

Spiritual Governance

Leadership, Jamaat & Discipline

Current Pir Pagaro

Syed Sibghatullah Shah Rashdi III, the 8th Pir Pagaro, continues the spiritual lineage of the Rashdi Khandan. He emphasizes devotion, education, and social welfare while maintaining the traditional values of the Jamaat.

Hur Jamaat

The Hur Jamaat represents a disciplined spiritual community that follows the teachings of the Rashdi family. It focuses on unity, service, and moral guidance for all its members.

Discipline & Regulations

Darghah Sharif maintains strict spiritual and social regulations. Followers observe discipline in education, religious practices, and community service, ensuring the legacy of loyalty, sacrifice, and enlightenment continues.

Current Leadership

Darghah Sharif

Darghah Sharif, under the guidance of the current Pir Pagaro, inspires devotion, discipline, and community service. His leadership balances tradition with modern education and social welfare.

He is the guardian of the Jamaat’s welfare and a symbol of unity for millions worldwide.

Current Spiritual Head

Syed Sibghatullah Shah Rashdi III

The 8th Pir Pagaro continues the spiritual lineage of the Rashdi Khandan, blending traditional guidance with modern education and social welfare. His leadership emphasizes discipline, devotion, and community development.
